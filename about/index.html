About_
LEAPX is a cross-disciplinary team based in the Silicon Valley of hardware: Shenzhen. By understanding and integrating technology with human interaction, we define and design revolutionary products that excel in form, function, and experience.
Founder_
Deng Yumian
After studying Mechanical Engineering and Visual Arts at Duke University and working at Microsoft, Yumian was recruited by DJI Founder Frank Wang to lead the design and development effort of Mavic Pro and Mavic Air, which when launched, changed the consumer drone industry forever with their extreme portability and performance. The Mavic series is widely recognized as the best in its class by consumers and medias alike, and was selected as the best 25 inventions of 2016 by Time magazine. In order to explore more innovation opportunities in other industries, Yumian founded LeapX Design.
