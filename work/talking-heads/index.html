Talking Heads Speaker
As a music lover, I have both speakers and headphone on my desk. But when the headphones are not in use, leaving them on the table looks messy, and a headphone stand takes too much desk space— I want to keep my desk clean and minimal. One day it occurred to me: why not hang the headphone on the speaker?

When I place the headset onto the desktop speaker, the lifeless speaker suddenly appeared to have a character and personality.

Minimalism designs are everywhere. They look great, but sometimes they’re also a little plain and emotionless.

Some humor and humanity is a nice change of pace. With this in mind, we set out to design the LEAPX Speaker: functional, beautiful, and a little humanity that makes you smile.
